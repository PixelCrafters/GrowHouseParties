The folks from Launch Party have teamed with Vancouver Pixel Crafters & Dealmaker Media to once again host the official kickoff party for Grow Conf 2013. But this year, we are taking things to a whole new level.

On August 14, we are taking over the hottest bars and restaurants in the historic Gastown area, home to Vancouver’s tech scene for a sprawling party. Regions from across Canada and the Pacific NW will each host their very own “House Party” to show off the very best their technology community has to offer. All houses will be within walking distance so attendees can easily move from house-to-house.

Who doesn’t love a good house party?

Channeling the international hospitality houses of the Vancouver 2010 Olympics and the branded parties of SXSW, House Party@GROW is a first-of-its kind multi-venue event designed to showcase the best technology hubs.

What does House Ownership include?

  • 1200+ attendees - all GrowConf attendees plus House Party only attendees from Vancouver and surroundings
  • all ticket sales run centrally
  • volunteers at each venue to handle the door & check in tickets
  • wristbands for all the attendees
  • custom printed passport with your house name, sponsors, & startups listed
  • interactive online map with your house name, sponsors, and startups listed
  • signage at each venue
  • street banners across the major streets in Gastown
  • SMS voting for all the houses & startups

If you are interesting in running a house, please contact us.
