The folks from Launch Party have teamed with Vancouver Pixel Crafters & Dealmaker Media to once again host the official kickoff party for Grow Conf 2013. But this year, we are taking things to a whole new level. Channeling the international hospitality houses of the Vancouver 2010 Olympics and the branded parties of SXSW, House Party@GROW is a first-of-its kind multi-venue event designed to showcase the best technology hubs.

What does House Ownership include?

  • 1200+ attendees - all GrowConf attendees plus House Party only attendees
  • all ticket sales run centrally
  • volunteers at each venue to handle the door & check in tickets
  • wristbands for all the attendees
  • custom printed map / passport with your house name & startups lists
  • interactive map with your house name, sponsors, and startups listed; all attendees will see it, it will be the main site promoted around the event, and we'll have some interaction around votes & checkins
  • signage at each venue
  • street banners across the major streets in Gastown
  • running SMS voting for all the houses participating, with prizes, media, and investor meetings for top startups.
